Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

Introduction

Moto Fluffs ("we", "our", "us") operates the motofluffs.com website and associated social media accounts. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information from visitors to this website. We do not use cookies, analytics trackers, or any form of user tracking on this site.

Third-Party Services

Our application integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Pinterest API — used to publish and manage pins on our own Pinterest account. We only access our own account data; we do not access any other user's Pinterest data.
  • Instagram Graph API — used to publish content to our own Instagram account (@motofluffs).

We do not collect, store, or share any third-party user data from these platforms.

Data We Access via Pinterest API

Through the Pinterest API, we access only:

  • Our own account information (profile, boards)
  • Our own pin data (creation, analytics)

We do not access any other Pinterest user's data, pins, boards, or personal information.

Data Storage

API access tokens are stored securely in local environment files and are never committed to public repositories or shared with third parties.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at: hello@motofluffs.com (or via Instagram @motofluffs).
